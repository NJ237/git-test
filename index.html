    Our Story

    Raising Puppies With Purpose & Love

    We believe every dog deserves the best start in life — and every family deserves a healthy, happy companion.

    👨‍👩‍👧‍👦
    Our Founder

    A Family Passion Turned Into a Calling

    My name is Margaret Wilson, and I've been raising dogs my entire life. What started as a family tradition of keeping golden retrievers in our farmhouse in Colorado has grown into a full-time calling.

    Over 15 years ago, I noticed how hard it was for families to find puppies from breeders who truly prioritized health and temperament over profit. That's what drives everything we do at Golden Paws.

    Every litter is planned carefully, socialized from day one, and health-tested before ever leaving our care. We don't just sell puppies — we match them with families we know will love them for life.

    🏥

    Health First

    Every puppy is examined by our licensed vet before going home. We test for genetic conditions and provide full health documentation.

    🌱

    Ethical Breeding

    We limit litters per year to ensure quality over quantity. Our parent dogs are AKC registered, well-loved, and never over-bred.

    🏡

    Home Raised

    Puppies grow up inside our home with our family and children — not in kennels. Early socialization makes all the difference.

    📋

    Full Transparency

    We share complete health records, vaccination history, and vet reports with every family. No surprises, ever.

    🤝

    Lifetime Support

    We're here after the purchase too. Questions about training, diet, or health? We answer every message personally.

    💕

    Love Guaranteed

    If your life circumstances change and you can no longer keep the puppy, we always accept them back. No dog left behind.

    Our Health Promise

    🩺

    Vet Certified

    Full health check by a licensed vet before pick-up, with written certification provided.

    💉

    Age-Appropriate Vaccines

    All core vaccines administered on schedule, with records provided at pick-up.

    🧬

    Genetic Testing

    🛡️

    We stand behind the health of every puppy with a written 2-year guarantee.
